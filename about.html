About Us

Learn more about Kelkar Clinic and our commitment to your health

Our Doctor

At Kelkar Clinic, we take pride in having an experienced and dedicated healthcare professional at the helm. Our doctor brings years of clinical experience and a patient-centered approach to medical care.

Dr. Kelkar is committed to providing personalized healthcare solutions to each patient. With a focus on preventive care and holistic treatment approaches, we ensure that every patient receives the attention and care they deserve.

Our doctor continuously updates their knowledge with the latest medical advancements to provide the best possible care to our patients.

Our Clinic

Kelkar Clinic has been serving the Malgund community with dedication and excellence. Our clinic is equipped with modern facilities and maintains strict hygiene standards to ensure the safety and well-being of our patients.

Located conveniently near the Post Office in Malgund, our clinic provides easy access to quality healthcare for residents of Ratnagiri and surrounding areas.

We believe in creating a comfortable and welcoming environment where patients can feel at ease while receiving treatment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to every member of our community.

We strive to build lasting relationships with our patients based on trust, respect, and excellent medical care. Your health and well-being are our top priorities.

At Kelkar Clinic, we believe that good health is the foundation of a happy life, and we're committed to helping you achieve and maintain it.
